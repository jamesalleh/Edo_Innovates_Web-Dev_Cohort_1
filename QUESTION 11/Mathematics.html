Course: Mathematics


The study of real numbers and their operations and properties;
order of operations including exponents and roots;
linear equations and inequalities in one and two variables,
their systems and applications; introduction to functions and graphs;
and the study of polynomials and their operations.
Earns credit toward graduation but will not satisfy any of the
six hours of Mathematics in the General Education.
