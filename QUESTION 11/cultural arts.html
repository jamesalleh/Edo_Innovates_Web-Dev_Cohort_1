Course: Cultural Arts


Cultural studies can be defined as a field of academia that examines
how meaning is created in social structures with adherence to class,
ethnicity, gender, race, ideology, nationality, etc.
The field draws from sociology, anthropology, politics, history,
economics, philosophy, literature, communications, and more
